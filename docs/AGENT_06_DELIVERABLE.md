## Planet 1: Inferna Prime ðŸ”¥

**Theme**: Volcanic Death World  
**Difficulty**: Medium  
**Best For**: Aggressive racers, heavy vehicles

### Track Layout: **"Magma Run"**

#### Lap Structure (50 seconds target)
1. **Start/Finish Straight** (8s)
   - Wide launch area
   - Item box at 5s mark
   - Gentle right curve into...

2. **Lava Falls** (12s) *SIGNATURE SECTION*
   - Sharp left hairpin around active volcano
   - Lava geysers erupt every 3 seconds (2s warning)
   - Inside line = faster but riskier (geyser timing)
   - Outside line = safer, lose 1.5s
   - Item box after hairpin

3. **Obsidian Canyon** (15s)
   - Narrow S-curves through black rock formations
   - Falling rocks from ceiling (random lanes, 0.8s telegraph)
   - Perfect drift section (chain 3 boosts = 3s advantage)
   - No item boxes (test skill)

4. **Caldera Jump** (10s) *SIGNATURE SECTION*
   - Ascending ramp toward volcano rim
   - Jump over lava lake
   - Three landing zones:
     - **Left**: Safe, but slowest (+1.0s)
     - **Center**: Standard landing
     - **Right**: Shortcut platform (saves 2s, hard to hit)
   - Item box immediately after landing

5. **Ash Cloud Straightaway** (5s)
   - Final sprint to finish
   - Visibility reduced (aesthetic only)
   - Boost zone in center

### Environmental Hazards

| Hazard | Frequency | Damage Type | Telegraph | Counter |
|--------|-----------|-------------|-----------|---------|
| Lava Geysers | Every 3s (cycle) | 2.0s respawn | 2.0s bubbling | Timing, shield |
| Falling Rocks | Random (30% chance per section) | 0.8s stun | 0.8s shadow | Dodge, shield |
| Lava Pools | Static (track edges) | 2.0s respawn | Glowing red | Don't fall off |
| Heat Shimmer | Constant | Visual only | N/A | N/A |

### Track Variants

#### **Magma Run: Twilight**
- **Change**: Sunset lighting, cooler lava (blue/purple)
- **Hazard Adjustment**: Geysers erupt every 4s (easier timing)
- **Visual**: Stunning silhouette racing

#### **Magma Run: Reverse**
- **Change**: Race counter-clockwise
- **New Challenge**: Caldera Jump is downward (brake control test)
- **Obsidian Canyon becomes descent** (faster, scarier)

#### **Magma Run: Eruption Mode**
- **Change**: Volcano actively erupting
- **Hazard**: Meteor showers across entire track (dodgeable)
- **Risk/Reward**: More item boxes (5 total vs 3)

### Atmosphere & Aesthetics
- **Sky**: Dark red, ash clouds, distant eruptions
- **Lighting**: Orange glow from lava, dramatic shadows
- **Audio**: Rumbling, crackling fire, rock slides
- **Particles**: Embers, ash, heat distortion

### Recommended Vehicle: Iron Hauler
- Stable through narrow sections
- Absorbs rock hits better

## Planet 2: Verdantia-9 ðŸŒ¿

**Theme**: Overgrown Jungle Paradise  
**Difficulty**: Hard  
**Best For**: Technical drivers, drift specialists

### Track Layout: **"Canopy Sprint"**

#### Lap Structure (55 seconds target)
1. **Ancient Ruins Start** (10s)
   - Launch through crumbling temple archway
   - Vine-covered straightaway
   - Item box at 6s
   - Gentle left into...

2. **Vine Tunnel** (8s) *TECHNICAL SECTION*
   - Tight winding corridor through giant trees
   - Hanging vines reduce visibility (cosmetic)
   - Perfect drift test (4 consecutive corners)
   - No hazards, pure skill

3. **Waterfall Leap** (12s) *SIGNATURE SECTION*
   - Bridge collapses mid-lap (after 1st racer crosses)
   - Must jump waterfall gap
   - Two jump zones:
     - **Early Jump**: Safer angle, longer airtime (+0.5s)
     - **Late Jump**: Shorter gap, tighter landing (saves 0.5s)
   - Item box on landing platform

4. **Bioluminescent Grove** (15s) *SIGNATURE SECTION*
   - Nighttime forest section (glowing plants)
   - Four possible paths (all same length, different difficulty)
     - **Path A**: Wide, safe, no hazards
     - **Path B**: Moderate, 2 tree roots to dodge
     - **Path C**: Narrow, 3 tight turns, saves 1s
     - **Path D**: Hidden (requires wall-ride), saves 2s
   - Item box at path merge

5. **Overgrowth Slope** (10s)
   - Descending spiral around massive tree trunk
   - Slippery moss patches (reduced traction)
   - Final item box at 8s
   - Finish line at tree base

### Environmental Hazards

| Hazard | Frequency | Damage Type | Telegraph | Counter |
|--------|-----------|-------------|-----------|---------|
| Snapping Vines | Every 4s (cycle) | 1.0s snare | 1.0s rustling | Timing, cut with boost |
| Tree Roots | Static (track obstacles) | 0.5s bump | Visual (raised) | Dodge, drift around |
| Moss Patches | Static (green zones) | Reduced grip | Color change | Avoid or counter-steer |
| Waterfall Mist | Constant (cosmetic) | Visual only | N/A | N/A |

### Track Variants

#### **Canopy Sprint: Monsoon**
- **Change**: Heavy rain, waterfalls more intense
- **Hazard Adjustment**: Moss patches everywhere (50% more slippery)
- **Visual**: Rain effects, puddles, lightning flashes

#### **Canopy Sprint: Reverse**
- **Change**: Race opposite direction
- **New Challenge**: Overgrowth Slope becomes steep ascent
- **Bioluminescent Grove exits different** (Path D harder to find)

#### **Canopy Sprint: Dawn Bloom**
- **Change**: Sunrise, flowers blooming animation
- **Hazard**: Pollen clouds reduce visibility in sections
- **Visual**: Golden lighting, beautiful but distracting

### Atmosphere & Aesthetics
- **Sky**: Green canopy ceiling, rays of light
- **Lighting**: Dappled sunlight, bioluminescent glow
- **Audio**: Bird calls, rustling leaves, waterfall roar
- **Particles**: Leaves, pollen, mist

### Recommended Vehicle: Void Ripper
- Excellent handling for tight turns
- Drift mastery rewarded heavily

## Planet 3: Cryon Drift â„ï¸

**Theme**: Frozen Wasteland  
**Difficulty**: Easy  
**Best For**: Speed demons, beginners

### Track Layout: **"Glacier Circuit"**

#### Lap Structure (45 seconds target)
1. **Ice Shelf Launch** (12s)
   - Long straightaway on frozen ocean
   - Perfect for top speed vehicles
   - Gentle right curve
   - Item box at 8s

2. **Iceberg Slalom** (10s) *TECHNICAL SECTION*
   - Weave between floating icebergs
   - Slippery ice = drift-friendly
   - Colliding with icebergs = 1.0s stun
   - Item box mid-section

3. **Aurora Tunnel** (8s) *SIGNATURE SECTION*
   - Ice cave with aurora borealis visible through ceiling
   - Frozen floor = automatic drift (no brake needed)
   - Gentle curves, speed-focused
   - Most beautiful section of all tracks

4. **Crevasse Bridge** (10s) *HAZARD SECTION*
   - Narrow ice bridge over deep chasm
   - Cracks appear randomly (30% chance per lap)
   - Cracked sections collapse if touched (2.0s respawn)
   - Telegraph: 1.5s warning (ice cracks visibly)
   - Shortcut: Jump over crevasse entirely (risky, saves 3s)

5. **Blizzard Straightaway** (5s)
   - Final sprint to finish
   - Whiteout conditions (reduced visibility)
   - Boost pad in center lane
   - Item box at 3s mark

### Environmental Hazards

| Hazard | Frequency | Damage Type | Telegraph | Counter |
|--------|-----------|-------------|-----------|---------|
| Ice Patches | Constant (track surface) | Reduced grip | Visual (shiny) | Drift to control |
| Falling Icicles | Random (20% per section) | 0.8s stun | 1.0s shadow | Dodge, shield |
| Bridge Collapse | 30% chance per lap | 2.0s respawn | 1.5s cracking sound | Avoid cracks, jump |
| Blizzard Wind | Final section only | Visual only | N/A | N/A |

### Track Variants

#### **Glacier Circuit: Night Freeze**
- **Change**: Nighttime, aurora more vibrant
- **Hazard Adjustment**: Ice even more slippery (+20%)
- **Visual**: Stunning aurora display, star-filled sky

#### **Glacier Circuit: Reverse**
- **Change**: Race opposite direction
- **New Challenge**: Blizzard Straightaway becomes first section (visibility challenge)
- **Aurora Tunnel exit is uphill** (speed maintenance test)

#### **Glacier Circuit: Avalanche**
- **Change**: Mountains actively collapsing
- **Hazard**: Snowballs roll across track (dodgeable, 1.5s stun if hit)
- **Visual**: Dynamic environment destruction

### Atmosphere & Aesthetics
- **Sky**: Pale blue, aurora greens and purples
- **Lighting**: Bright white ice, soft aurora glow
- **Audio**: Wind howling, ice cracking, ethereal hums
- **Particles**: Snowflakes, ice crystals, aurora shimmer

### Recommended Vehicle: Photon Dart
- Maximizes straightaway advantage
- Slippery ice suits high-speed play

## Performance Optimization (Mobile)

### Level of Detail (LOD)
- **Near**: Full detail (trees, rocks, particles)
- **Medium** (30â€“50 units): Reduced polygons (50%)
- **Far** (50+ units): Billboards and low-poly (80% reduction)

### Track-Specific Optimizations
- **Inferna Prime**: Lava shader simplified on low-end devices
- **Verdantia-9**: Foliage density scales with performance mode
- **Cryon Drift**: Particle count reduced (snow, aurora)

### Target Frame Rates
- **High-end** (iPhone 12+): 60 FPS
- **Mid-range** (iPhone X): 30â€“45 FPS
- **Low-end** (iPhone SE): 30 FPS minimum
